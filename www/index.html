Your Tracking tab shows the current step and estimated ready time. We also notify you when the order becomes Ready.
Can I change my pickup time?
Open your order details and contact support. We can usually adjust the pickup window if the driver hasn’t arrived yet.
What if I’m missing an item?
Send us a message with your order number and a description. We’ll investigate and follow up.
